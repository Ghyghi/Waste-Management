The types of wastes

Recyclable Waste
These are wastes that can be recycled. They include paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
Non-Recyclabe Waste
These are wastes that cannot be recycled. They include food waste, used tissues, and sanitary towels.
General Waste
These are wastes that do not fit into the other categories. They include broken glass, ceramics, and mirrors.
